# ABILITY FLAVOR TEXT - Vale Chronicles

**Story Director Deliverable - Phase 2**

This document contains atmospheric flavor text for all Psynergy abilities across all four elements.

---

# WHAT IS PSYNERGY?

Psynergy is the manifestation of elemental power through mental and spiritual focus. Each element flows differently, shaped by the user's will and personality. These abilities range from simple elemental manipulation to devastating displays of raw power.

**Note:** This document provides FLAVOR ONLY - no damage numbers, stat requirements, or mechanical details. That's for the Architect.

---

**Visual Style:** Stone, earth, crystals, mountains, tremors

# DESIGN NOTES

**For Graphics Team:**
- Each element has distinct visual identity
- Color coding: Venus (Brown/Green), Mars (Red/Orange), Mercury (Blue/Cyan), Jupiter (Yellow/Purple)
- Visual effects should feel impactful but not obscure gameplay
- Higher tier abilities = more spectacular visuals

**For Sound Team:**
- Each element needs distinct sound profile
- Earth: Deep, rumbling, heavy
- Fire: Crackling, explosive, intense
- Water: Flowing, splashing, with crystalline ice sounds
- Wind: Whistling, crackling (lightning), rushing air

**For Architect:**
- Flavor text indicates general function (damage, healing, buff, etc.)
- Mechanical implementation is your domain
- Balance around player experience, not lore power levels

# ABILITY NAMING PHILOSOPHY

**Tier 1:** Simple, descriptive names (Quake, Flare, Frost, Gust)
**Tier 2:** Evocative, powerful names (Earthquake, Inferno, Tidal Wave, Hurricane)
**Tier 3:** Legendary, ultimate names (Judgment, Apocalypse, Leviathan's Wrath, Tempest)

**Voice Lines:**
- Short and punchy
- Character personality shows through delivery
- Some abilities share similar lines but different tones
