# STARTER UNITS - Vale Chronicles

**Story Director Deliverable - Phase 1**

This document contains complete character profiles for the 3 starter units players can choose from.

---

## UNIT 1: Isaac (Starter Choice A)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Isaac
- **Element:** Venus (Earth)
- **Age:** 17
- **Hometown:** Vale Village
- **Base Class:** Squire → Earth Adept (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Longsword

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Brave, determined, compassionate yet haunted by loss
- **Speech Pattern:** Direct and honest. Short sentences. "Let's go" not "Perhaps we should consider going"
- **Motivation:** Prove himself worthy of his father's legacy. His father died protecting Vale years ago, and Isaac feels the weight of living up to that sacrifice
- **Fears:** Failing those who depend on him, letting his mother down
- **Relationship to Player:** The noble protagonist. Natural leader type who inspires loyalty through actions, not words

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Balanced Warrior (Tank/DPS hybrid)
- **Strengths:** High HP, strong physical attacks, earth Psynergy for offense and defense
- **Weaknesses:** No healing abilities, medium speed, vulnerable to wind attacks
- **Playstyle:** Frontline fighter who can absorb punishment while dealing consistent damage

### Abilities by Level (Flavor Only)
- **Level 1: Slash**
  - *Flavor:* "A straightforward sword strike. Nothing fancy, but Isaac's training shows through in his form and precision."

- **Level 2: Quake**
  - *Flavor:* "Isaac channels earth energy through his blade into the ground, causing tremors that destabilize all enemies. The earth itself becomes his weapon."

- **Level 3: Clay Spire**
  - *Flavor:* "Stone pillars erupt from beneath enemies, impaling them with the fury of the earth. Isaac's father used this technique to defend Vale."

- **Level 4: Ragnarok**
  - *Flavor:* "A massive blade of pure earth energy materializes in the sky and crashes down with devastating force. The signature move of Vale's greatest warriors."

- **Level 5: Judgment**
  - *Flavor:* "The ultimate earth technique. A pillar of divine earth power descends from the heavens, channeling the spirits of all who defended Vale before. Isaac's father's legacy made manifest."

### Dialogue Samples

**When First Met (Tutorial NPC):**
> "I'm Isaac. My father was a warrior who protected this village until the day he fell. Now it's my turn to prove myself. Will you help me train? I need to get stronger."

**After Recruitment:**
> "Together we'll protect Vale from whatever darkness threatens it. My father would have wanted me to fight alongside strong companions."

**In Battle (Low HP):**
> "Not yet... I can't fall here. Vale still needs me!"

**In Battle (Critical Hit):**
> "For Vale! For my father!"

**In Battle (Using Level 5 Ability):**
> "I call upon the earth itself! Judgment!"

**When Djinn Equipped:**
> "I can feel the power of the earth flowing through me. This is what my father must have felt."

**After Victory:**
> "We did it. But there's more to do. Let's keep moving."

**In Vale Village (Dialogue NPC):**
> "This village raised me. Every face here is someone I'd protect with my life."

### Recruitment Method
- **Source:** Starter choice (player picks 1 of 3 starters)
- **Location:** Vale Village Center - Tutorial Area
- **Level When Joined:** 1
- **Recruitment Flag:** `starter_selected_isaac`
- **Requirements:** None (tutorial character)
- **Recruitment Dialogue:**
  > "You want me on your team? I've been waiting for a chance like this. Let's show everyone what we can do together!"

## UNIT 2: Garet (Starter Choice B)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Garet
- **Element:** Mars (Fire)
- **Age:** 18
- **Hometown:** Vale Village
- **Base Class:** Fighter → Flame Adept (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Battle Axe

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Passionate, loyal, impulsive, heart of gold
- **Speech Pattern:** Energetic and enthusiastic. Uses exclamations. "Let's do this!" Energy in every sentence
- **Motivation:** Protect his friends and prove that strength comes from the heart, not just training
- **Fears:** Being useless, watching friends get hurt while he stands by
- **Relationship to Player:** The loyal best friend. Always has your back, even when he disagrees with you

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Pure DPS (Glass Cannon Warrior)
- **Strengths:** Highest physical attack, fire Psynergy for massive burst damage, intimidating presence
- **Weaknesses:** Lower defense than Isaac, no support abilities, vulnerable to water attacks
- **Playstyle:** All-out offense. Garet hits hard and fast but needs teammates to cover his weaknesses

### Abilities by Level (Flavor Only)
- **Level 1: Cleave**
  - *Flavor:* "A powerful overhead axe strike that can split boulders. Garet puts his whole body into every swing."

- **Level 2: Fireball**
  - *Flavor:* "Conjures a sphere of roaring flame and hurls it at enemies. The heat is intense enough to scorch the air."

- **Level 3: Volcano**
  - *Flavor:* "Summons molten rock from deep underground to erupt beneath all foes. The battlefield becomes a hazard zone."

- **Level 4: Meteor Strike**
  - *Flavor:* "Calls down a meteor wreathed in flames. Where it impacts, nothing but ash remains."

- **Level 5: Pyroclasm**
  - *Flavor:* "The ultimate fire technique. An explosion of pure volcanic fury that engulfs everything. Garet's unbridled passion made real."

### Dialogue Samples

**When First Met (Training Grounds):**
> "Hey there! Name's Garet! My dad's Kyle, the Guard Captain. Want to test your skills against me? Don't worry, I'll go easy on you! ...Okay, maybe not THAT easy!"

**After Recruitment:**
> "Whoa, you're strong! Mind if I tag along? We could do amazing things together! Besides, someone's gotta watch your back!"

**In Battle (Low HP):**
> "Ugh... Okay, that hurt more than I expected. But I'm not done yet!"

**In Battle (Critical Hit):**
> "BURN! HAHA! Take that!"

**In Battle (Using Level 5 Ability):**
> "Time to turn up the heat! PYROCLASM!"

**When Djinn Equipped:**
> "Oh yeah! Now we're talking! I'm fired up and ready to go!"

**After Victory:**
> "YES! Did you see that?! We totally crushed them! Who's next?!"

**In Vale Village (Dialogue NPC):**
> "I love this place! Everyone here is like family. That's why we fight - to protect what matters!"

### Recruitment Method
- **Source:** Starter choice (player picks 1 of 3 starters)
- **Location:** Vale Village Center - Tutorial Area
- **Level When Joined:** 1
- **Recruitment Flag:** `starter_selected_garet`
- **Requirements:** None (tutorial character)
- **Recruitment Dialogue:**
  > "You picking me? AWESOME! Let's go burn through anyone who stands in our way! Metaphorically... well, maybe literally too!"

- **Age:** 16
- **Hometown:** Vale Village (recently arrived)

# SUMMARY: Starter Units

## The 3 Starter Choices:

1. **Isaac** - Venus/Earth Balanced Warrior
   - For players who want: Balanced offense/defense, straightforward playstyle, classic protagonist

2. **Garet** - Mars/Fire Pure DPS
   - For players who want: High damage output, aggressive playstyle, energetic personality

3. **Ivan** - Jupiter/Wind Elemental Mage
   - For players who want: Magical attacks, strategic gameplay, mysterious character

## Design Philosophy:
- **Different elements** ensure each starter offers unique gameplay from the beginning
- **Distinct personalities** make the choice feel meaningful beyond stats
- **Clear archetypes** (tank, DPS, mage) help players choose based on preferred playstyle
- **All viable** - no "trap" choice; each starter can succeed throughout the game

---

**Story Director Notes:**
- Each character has a complete, distinct personality
- Dialogue samples show voice consistency
- Abilities progress from basic (Level 1) to ultimate (Level 5)
- Recruitment is simple for tutorial phase
- No mechanical details - just flavor and narrative
