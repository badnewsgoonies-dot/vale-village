## UNIT 4: Mia (Recruitable - Early Game)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Mia
- **Element:** Mercury (Water/Ice)
- **Age:** 18
- **Hometown:** Imil (visiting Vale)
- **Base Class:** Cleric → Tide Adept (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Mace

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Gentle, compassionate, strong-willed, carries healer's burden
- **Speech Pattern:** Soft but firm. Caring tone. "Are you hurt?" before "Did we win?"
- **Motivation:** Master healing arts to prevent others from experiencing loss. Lost her mentor to illness she couldn't cure
- **Fears:** Being unable to save someone, becoming cold and distant like ice
- **Relationship to Player:** The compassionate healer. Reminds the party of their humanity amid battle

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Primary Healer + Ice Mage
- **Strengths:** Best healing abilities, defensive buffs, ice attacks, high magic defense
- **Weaknesses:** Low physical attack, slow speed, vulnerable to earth attacks
- **Playstyle:** Support-focused with defensive ice magic. Keeps party alive while controlling battlefield

### Abilities by Level (Flavor Only)
- **Level 1: Ply**
  - *Flavor:* "Gentle waters wash over wounds, knitting flesh and soothing pain. Mia's compassion flows through every drop."

- **Level 2: Frost**
  - *Flavor:* "Freezes the air itself into razor-sharp shards of ice that rain down on enemies. Beautiful and deadly."

- **Level 3: Ice Horn**
  - *Flavor:* "Summons a massive spear of ice that impales a single target. The cold is so intense it burns."

- **Level 4: Wish**
  - *Flavor:* "A prayer to the waters of the world. Healing energy flows to all allies, mending wounds and restoring hope."

- **Level 5: Glacial Blessing**
  - *Flavor:* "The ultimate water technique. Mia becomes a conduit for the healing power of all water in the world. Death itself must wait."

### Dialogue Samples

**When First Met (Vale Healing House):**
> "Hello. I'm Mia, a healer from Imil. I've come to Vale to study with the local physicians. I've heard you're quite talented. Would you... spar with me? I need to test my abilities."

**After Defeat (Recruitment):**
> "You're very strong. But I can see you care about not hurting others. Someone like you... needs a healer. I'd like to join you, if you'll have me."

**In Battle (Low HP):**
> "I need to... keep everyone safe. I can't fall now..."

**In Battle (Healing Ally):**
> "Hold on! I won't let you die!"

**In Battle (Using Level 5 Ability):**
> "Let the waters of life flow! Glacial Blessing!"

**When Djinn Equipped:**
> "The Djinni's power... it feels like a cool stream. Refreshing and pure."

**After Victory:**
> "Is everyone alright? Let me check your injuries before we continue."

**In Vale Village (Dialogue NPC):**
> "Vale is a beautiful place. The people here have such strong hearts. I'm glad I can help protect them."

### Recruitment Method
- **Source:** Early-game recruitable NPC
- **Location:** Vale Healing House (South District)
- **Level When Joined:** 3
- **Recruitment Flag:** `defeated_mia_friendly_spar`
- **Requirements:** Win friendly sparring match against Mia
- **Recruitment Dialogue:**
  > "I've seen how you fight - not with cruelty, but with purpose. Let me fight beside you. I promise I'll keep us all safe."

## UNIT 5: Felix (Recruitable - Mid Game)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Felix
- **Element:** Venus (Earth)
- **Age:** 19
- **Hometown:** Unknown (Wanderer)
- **Base Class:** Rogue → Stone Assassin (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Dual Daggers

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Mysterious, calculating, honorable beneath cold exterior, haunted by past
- **Speech Pattern:** Terse. Single words when possible. "No." "Fine." "Later." Opens up only to those he trusts
- **Motivation:** Atone for something in his past. He won't speak of it, but guilt drives every action
- **Fears:** Forming attachments, repeating past mistakes, being unable to change
- **Relationship to Player:** The dark reflection. Shows that even those with regrets can fight for good

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Rogue/Assassin (Speed-based DPS)
- **Strengths:** Highest speed, critical hit chance, evasion, earth Psynergy for burst damage
- **Weaknesses:** Low HP, moderate defense, requires positioning, vulnerable to wind attacks
- **Playstyle:** Strike-first assassin. Eliminates threats before they can act

### Abilities by Level (Flavor Only)
- **Level 1: Twin Strike**
  - *Flavor:* "A lightning-fast double slash with both daggers. Most enemies don't see the second strike coming."

- **Level 2: Stone Bullet**
  - *Flavor:* "Launches razor-sharp stone fragments at vulnerable points. Felix knows exactly where to strike."

- **Level 3: Sneak Attack**
  - *Flavor:* "Disappears into shadows and strikes from behind. By the time the enemy realizes, it's too late."

- **Level 4: Earthquake Stomp**
  - *Flavor:* "Channels earth energy through a devastating stomp that shatters the ground beneath all foes."

- **Level 5: Oblivion**
  - *Flavor:* "The ultimate assassination technique. Felix becomes one with the earth and stone, striking from impossible angles with lethal precision. His past made weapon."

### Dialogue Samples

**When First Met (Vale Outskirts):**
> "...You're the one they talk about. Strong. I need to test that. Fight me. Honor duel. No holding back."

**After Defeat (Recruitment):**
> "...Strong. Stronger than me. Fine. I'll join. But don't expect me to talk much."

**In Battle (Low HP):**
> "Tch... Not yet..."

**In Battle (Critical Hit):**
> "There."

**In Battle (Using Level 5 Ability):**
> "This ends now. Oblivion."

**When Djinn Equipped:**
> "...Good. More power. I'll need it."

**After Victory:**
> "Done. Move on."

**In Vale Village (Dialogue NPC) - After Trust Built:**
> "...This place. It's... peaceful. Reminds me of... before. Maybe I can protect this. For once."

### Recruitment Method
- **Source:** Mid-game recruitable NPC
- **Location:** Vale Outskirts - Northern Path
- **Level When Joined:** 5
- **Recruitment Flag:** `defeated_felix_honor_duel`
- **Requirements:** Win honor duel against Felix (1v1 battle)
- **Recruitment Dialogue:**
  > "You won. Fair and square. I respect strength. I'll fight with you. Just... don't ask about my past. Not yet."

## UNIT 6: Jenna (Recruitable - Mid Game)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Jenna
- **Element:** Mars (Fire)
- **Age:** 17
- **Hometown:** Vale Village
- **Base Class:** Fire Mage → Inferno Adept (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Wand

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Fiery, independent, protective, sharp-tongued with a soft heart
- **Speech Pattern:** Direct and sarcastic. Quick wit. "Oh sure, run headfirst into danger. What could go wrong?"
- **Motivation:** Prove she doesn't need rescuing. Tired of being seen as delicate
- **Fears:** Being helpless, becoming a burden, losing independence
- **Relationship to Player:** The tsundere. Acts tough but deeply cares about the party

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Elemental Mage (AoE Fire Specialist)
- **Strengths:** Highest magic attack, devastating fire AoE spells, can inflict burn status
- **Weaknesses:** Very low defense, no healing, vulnerable to water attacks
- **Playstyle:** Pure magical damage. Stands in back and incinerates everything

### Abilities by Level (Flavor Only)
- **Level 1: Flare**
  - *Flavor:* "A burst of flames engulfs a single target. Simple, effective, and Jenna's favorite way to make a point."

- **Level 2: Fire Bomb**

- **Level 3: Inferno**
  - *Flavor:* "Summons a raging firestorm that consumes all enemies. Nothing but cinders remain."

- **Level 4: Blast Burn**
  - *Flavor:* "Channels fire energy into an explosive beam that pierces through multiple targets. Jenna's power made manifest."

- **Level 5: Apocalypse**
  - *Flavor:* "The ultimate fire technique. The very air ignites as Jenna calls forth the end of all things in flame. Her fury given form."

### Dialogue Samples

**After Victory:**
> "See? THAT'S how it's done! Maybe you guys are learning from me after all."

**In Vale Village (Dialogue NPC):**
> "What? No, I don't miss Vale... Okay, fine, maybe a little. Don't tell anyone I said that."

### Recruitment Method

## UNIT 9: Kraden (Recruitable - Late Game)

### Basic Info
- **Name:** Kraden
- **Element:** None (Scholar - can equip any Djinn)
- **Age:** 54
- **Hometown:** Vale Village
- **Base Class:** Scholar → Alchemist (with Djinn)
- **Weapon:** Tome/Staff

### Personality
- **Core Traits:** Brilliant, absent-minded, curious to a fault, surprisingly brave when pushed
- **Speech Pattern:** Academic and verbose. "Fascinating! You see, the theoretical implications..." Can lecture mid-battle
- **Motivation:** Unlock the secrets of Alchemy. His life's work is understanding the ancient powers
- **Fears:** Dying before discovering the truth, his research being lost to time
- **Relationship to Player:** The eccentric mentor. Provides lore and context for everything happening

### Combat Role
- **Archetype:** Versatile Support (Adapts based on Djinn equipped)
- **Strengths:** Can use any element's Psynergy via Djinn, vast knowledge provides unique abilities, buffs entire party
- **Weaknesses:** Lowest combat stats, slow, physically weak, requires Djinn to be useful
- **Playstyle:** Adaptable support. Changes role based on party needs and equipped Djinn

- **Element:** Mars (Fire)
