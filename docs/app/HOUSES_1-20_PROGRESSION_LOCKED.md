## **DESIGN PHILOSOPHY**

### **Core Principles:**
1. **8 Djinn across 20 houses** (better pacing than original 12)
2. **Venus/Jupiter favored** (2 users each = equipment sharing benefits)
3. **Power spikes every 3-5 houses** (maintain player excitement)
4. **Permanent vs Temporary balance** (unit abilities never lost, Djinn abilities conditional)
5. **Quality over quantity** (11 powerful abilities > 20 weak ones)

```

‚ö†Ô∏è LOST when Djinn on cooldown (4 rounds!)
```
