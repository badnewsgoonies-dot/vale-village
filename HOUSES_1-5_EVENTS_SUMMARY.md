## üè† HOUSE 1: Garet's Liberation

### Battle Details
- **Enemy:** `garet-enemy` (War Mage enemy version - single enemy)
- **Difficulty:** Easy (first battle/tutorial)
- **Rewards:**
  - 60 XP
  - 20 gold
  - Wooden Sword (equipment)
  - **Forge Djinn** (Mars T1 Djinn)

### Story Events
- **Pre-Battle:** Earth Scout taunts Isaac about controlling a wolf
- **Battle:** Fight against War Mage enemy (Garet's corrupted version?)
- **Post-Battle:** 
  - War Mage (Garet) approaches and joins the team
  - Garet gives Forge Djinn to Isaac
  - **First unit recruitment from battle!**

### Key Points for Dialogue
- First house liberated
- Isaac discovers creatures are being controlled
- Garet is impressed and wants to join
- Forge Djinn is Mars element (fire/earth theme)
- Milestone: First recruit!

## üè† HOUSE 2: The Bronze Trial

### Battle Details
- **Enemies:** `earth-scout` + `venus-wolf` (2 enemies)
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Rewards:**
  - 70 XP
  - 22 gold
  - Bronze Sword (Venus equipment)

### Story Events
- **Pre-Battle:** Flame Scout taunts Isaac about fire
- **Battle:** Fight earth scout and venus wolf
- **Post-Battle:**
  - **Mystic** (Mercury element) arrives and joins automatically
  - Story join (not from battle reward, triggered by story flag)
  - Mystic has been watching Isaac's progress

### Key Points for Dialogue
- Second house liberated
- Fire vs Earth theme (Flame Scout vs Isaac's earth magic)
- Mystic is Mercury element (water/ice theme)
- Story join (not recruited from battle, but narrative-driven)
- No Djinn granted here
